# Result Institute Brand Colors

Extracted from the official logo (`logo.png`)

## Primary Colors

### Magenta (Result)
- **Hex:** `#E91E8C`
- **RGB:** `rgb(233, 30, 140)`
- **Usage:** Primary brand color, buttons, accents, "Result" branding

### Blue (Institute)
- **Hex:** `#4A7FC1`
- **RGB:** `rgb(74, 127, 193)`
- **Usage:** Secondary brand color, links, "institute" branding, headers

## Neutral Colors

### Dark Charcoal
- **Hex:** `#2D3436`
- **RGB:** `rgb(45, 52, 54)`
- **Usage:** Primary text, headings, dark UI elements

### White
- **Hex:** `#FFFFFF`
- **RGB:** `rgb(255, 255, 255)`
- **Usage:** Backgrounds, cards, light text on dark backgrounds

## Usage Guidelines

### Primary Actions
- Background: Magenta `#E91E8C`
- Text: White `#FFFFFF`
- Hover: Light Magenta `#FF6BB5`

### Secondary Actions
- Background: Blue `#4A7FC1`
- Text: White `#FFFFFF`
- Hover: Light Blue `#6FA3E0`

### Text Hierarchy
- Headings: Dark Charcoal `#2D3436`
- Body Text: Gray `#636E72`
- Links: Blue `#4A7FC1`

### Status Colors

#### Success
- **Green:** `#00B894`
- Usage: Success messages, completed tasks

#### Warning
- **Orange:** `#FDCB6E`
- Usage: Warnings, pending states

#### Error
- **Red:** `#D63031`
- Usage: Error messages, validation errors

#### Info
- **Blue:** `#4A7FC1` (brand blue)
- Usage: Information messages, tooltips
